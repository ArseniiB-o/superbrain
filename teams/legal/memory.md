# Team Memory: legal
Last updated: 2026-03-26

## Accumulated Knowledge
- GDPR: always ask if personal data is processed — if yes, full compliance required
- EU AI Act: high-risk AI systems need conformity assessment before market
- UK Ltd vs GmbH: UK cheaper to set up, GmbH more credible in DACH market
- Cookie consent: explicit opt-in required in EU, pre-ticked boxes = illegal
- IP: always clarify who owns IP when contractors are involved
- Data residency: EU customer data must stay in EU by default (GDPR Chapter V)
