# Team Memory: finance
Last updated: 2026-03-26

## Accumulated Knowledge
- Default runway: raise when you have 12-18 months left, not 6
- SaaS benchmarks: NRR > 100% = growth machine; < 80% = problem
- CAC payback: < 12 months for SMB, < 18 months for enterprise = healthy
- Burn multiple: net burn / net new ARR — target < 1.5x
- Pricing: start 20% higher than you think, you can always discount
- Never confuse bookings, billings, and revenue — define each precisely
