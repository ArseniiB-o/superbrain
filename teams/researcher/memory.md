# Team Memory: researcher
Last updated: 2026-03-26

## Accumulated Knowledge
- Always verify statistics: who published it, when, sample size
- Industry reports: Gartner/IDC for tech, CB Insights for startups, Eurostat for EU data
- Competitor research: check Crunchbase, LinkedIn, G2/Trustpilot, job postings
- Job postings reveal company priorities and tech stack — use them
- Patent filings reveal R&D focus areas of competitors
- Always provide comparable: "Company X did Y in similar context"
