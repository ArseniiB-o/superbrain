# Team Memory: risk
Last updated: 2026-03-26

## Accumulated Knowledge
- Risk matrix: score = probability × impact (1-25 scale)
- Critical threshold: score ≥ 15 = immediate mitigation required
- Top startup killers: running out of cash, founder conflict, wrong market timing
- Technical risks often underestimated: always add 3x time buffer for unknowns
- Regulatory risk: most startups discover compliance requirements too late
- Single points of failure: always identify and document, never ignore
